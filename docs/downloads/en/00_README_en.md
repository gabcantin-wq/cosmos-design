## What is this?

This repository contains the **structured extraction** of a cosmological model intuitively carried by Gabriel Cantin since 2006, formalized in extended sessions between April 24 and May 7, 2026.

The model proposes an **alternative structural reading** of the universe — not a correction to the Standard Model of physics, but a self-contained reading built from a few fundamental pieces: a single **e** (unique energetic entity) at **t=0**, a circular and constant **T** axis, a generative function **4df(x)**, and the back-and-forth duality of energy-links.

The corpus is organized to allow **a mathematician or theoretical physicist** (human or advanced LLM) to take over for **Phase 2 — mathematical formalization**.

## Five fundamental structural pieces (ultra-condensed view)

1. **The unique e** resides at **t=0**, omnipresent, out of sequence.
2. **T is circular and constant** — all "moments" coexist as points on this axis.
3. **4df(x) is an integral operator** over the path between **t=x** (our observation membrane) and **t=0**.
4. **Energy-links** are the structural corridors ("glue corridors") that address e within t=x — canonical image: "stretched eraser".
5. **Dark repulsion is the unifying structural mechanism**. Concentrated at t=0+1, diluted at t=x, it acts on the back-and-forth duality of energy-links and determines the partition between **mass** and **displacement** (two representations of the same 4df(x) output). c (speed limit) emerges from this repulsion as an extremal equilibrium at t=0. Mass is not the cause of slowness — **slowness (structural impediment) causes mass**.

To understand why these 5 sentences are not absurd but structurally coherent, read `01_BOOTSTRAP.md` and `03_MINIMAL_STRUCTURAL_CHAIN.md`.
