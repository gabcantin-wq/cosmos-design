## 1. Fundamental postulates

### Postulate I `[POSTULAT]` — Register distinction between t=0 and t=x
t=0 is an ontological register distinct from observable space-time (t=x). The physical laws valid in t=x do not apply to t=0. The quantities of t=x (spatial distance in meters, duration in seconds, density, direction) have no meaning applied to t=0.

*Illustration*: pointing to "the direction of the Big Bang" has no meaning.

*Causal hierarchy*: without distance, no space. Without space, no time.

*Fundamental open question (A vs B)*:
- **Reading A**: t=0 is a real and distinct register; t=x is a second plane connected to t=0 by weaving.
- **Reading B**: there is only one plane (T); t=0 is the fundamental state of energy, not a separate place.
- Current preference: **B**. Reinforced by the 2026-04-24 session: t=0 is ambient and unique (no plurality of t=0), without internal metric, everywhere simultaneously, out of sequence. Not formally settled, but the coherence of the model leans clearly toward B.

### Postulate II `[POSTULAT]` — Duality of energy (with spectrum)
All energy within t=x exists in one of two modes, but bottling is not binary — it is a **spectrum of impediment**:

- **Free energy**: weaving fully expressed, no impediment. Velocity c, direction of creation vector. Example: photon.
- **Bottled energy**: weaving impeded, in varying degrees.
  - Weakly impeded (e.g.: neutrino) — tiny mass.
  - Moderately impeded (e.g.: electron) — small mass.
  - Strongly impeded (e.g.: quark, proton) — significant mass.

Ontological category. The modes interconvert (pair creation, annihilation).

**Internal structure**: bottled energy carries an internal configuration (bound quarks, leptonic properties) that can be inverted → antimatter. Fully expressed free energy has no structure to invert → no distinct antiparticle.

### Postulate III `[POSTULAT]` — Universal weaving and its manifestations
Every unit of energy within t=x maintains a **link (weaving)** with t=0. Not a force — a structural relation.

- **Free energy**: weaving fully expressed as displacement at c. The weaving **co-travels** with the unit.
- **Bottled energy**: weaving impeded. The unit cannot ascend/recede against t; it remains stuck within t=x. Its weaving, however, propagates. **The maintained tension is gravity.**

**Precision (2026-04-24)**: weaving exists for both modes. The distinction concerns the relationship between the weaving and the unit that carries it:
- Free: unit and weaving advance together at c within t=x. The motif does not stagnate at one point — it moves with its carrier.
- Bottled: the unit is trapped within t=x, the weaving stays in place and radiates fully. This stationary residue produces the observable gravitational signature.

### Postulate IV `[POSTULAT]` — Uniqueness of energy and addressing
The energy of the universe is **unique** at t=0. The multiplicity perceived within t=x = multiplicity of **addresses**, not entities.

e.T = sum of all e.t addressed within t=x.

### Postulate V `[POSTULAT]` — Distancing toward t=0 and origin of forces
Every unit of energy has a **distancing toward t=0** = the addressing vector.

**Every force within t=x is a manifestation of weaving/distancing.** Gravity = impediment. EM, strong, weak = manifestations to specify (see Postulate IX for unification by degree of impediment).

### Postulate VI `[POSTULAT]` — Single mechanism of affliction of bottled energy
Bottled energy is normally in a state of impeded weaving. When subjected to certain extreme conditions, its impeded weaving is **pushed toward its limit state** (full expression, close to free regime, approach to t=0).

**A single fundamental mechanism**, several manifestations:
- **Moderate energy concentration** (beta+ decay, absorbed gamma photon) → internal reorganization, emergence of new addresses.
- **Extreme energy concentration** (accelerator) → local reproduction of t=0 conditions → matter-antimatter pair creation.
- **Velocity at c** → extreme time dilation (proper time tends to zero).
- **Gravitational collapse** (black hole) → the weaving yields, the energy returns to t=0.

### Postulate VII `[POSTULAT]` — Mechanism of regeneration of the addressing vector (hash/seed)
Each unit of energy within t=x has a unique **seed** — its addressing vector inherited from its creation.

When a unit generates a new unit by interaction, the regeneration algorithm takes as input the **source seed** and the **event conditions**, and produces the new addressing vector of the emergent unit.

**The operation is irreversible** (like a cryptographic hash). One cannot trace the source back from the addressing vector of the child. The information that links parent and child exists only at t=0, not within t=x.

**Consequences**:
- Two units coming from different sources have different addressings by nature.
- Entanglement (common root at t=0) does not allow communication within t=x.
- The "limitations" of entanglement = conditions of a shared creation event.

### Postulate VIII `[POSTULAT]` — Intrinsic oscillation of weaving (added 2026-04-24, deepened 2026-04-27)

**The weaving within t=x is not static: it oscillates intrinsically.** Impediment and propagation are dynamic regimes of this oscillation, not frozen states.

**Deepening (2026-04-27) — oscillation IS the back-and-forth t=x ↔ t=0.** A complete oscillation = a back-and-forth. The frequency ν = number of back-and-forths per unit of time. The period τ = 1/ν = duration of a back-and-forth. This formulation unifies Postulate III (the weaving is the link to t=0) and Postulate VIII (the weaving oscillates): they are two descriptions of the same phenomenon.

- **Free weaving (photon)**: oscillation **and** propagation are the same phenomenon. The weaving oscillates while moving forward at c. The **frequency of the photon is directly the observable weaving**, because nothing retains it. While a back-and-forth occurs, the carrier advances by c·τ — this is the **wavelength λ = c/ν**, trace within t=x of a back-and-forth.
- **Bottled weaving (electron, etc.)**: oscillation continues, but propagation is impeded. The weaving oscillates **in place** — back-and-forths without advancing. The intrinsic frequency corresponds to the **Compton frequency** (ν = m·c²/h) — Zitterbewegung from RQM (Dirac 1930) is the direct theoretical observation of this oscillation.

**Structural corollaries**:
- Bottling is never total. Even the most bound electron permanently keeps a portion of weaving that grazes liberation — it is this portion that can become a photon in case of reorganization.
- The **discrete energy levels** (atomic orbitals, nuclei) are **self-coherent** oscillation configurations of bottled weaving. Other configurations destabilize and converge toward the closest stable one.
- **Bound systems** (atoms, molecules, nuclei) are **coupled and synchronized oscillations** of multiple bottled weavings.
- The **rest mass** is the measure of **average** impediment over an oscillation cycle.
- **Inertia** is the rigidity of weaving anchoring against re-addressing — direct manifestation of oscillatory impediment. It and gravitational mass have the same origin, which realizes the equivalence principle by identity, not by coincidence.

### Postulate IX `[POSTULAT]` — Unification of forces by degree of impediment (added 2026-04-24, refined 2026-04-27)

**The four fundamental interactions are manifestations of weaving — but they are not all the same mechanism.** Refinement 2026-04-27: there are in reality **two structurally distinct mechanisms**, grouped under the word "force" by historical convention.

**Mechanism A — Sharing of access to t=0 between close bottled.**
Two bottled weavings in durable proximity within t=x share access to the same region of t=0. The closer they are within t=x, the more their back-and-forths converge, the stronger the coupled self-coherence. This is what produces:
- The **strong force** (quarks within a hadron) — extreme sharing, requires proximity.
- The **weak force** (flavor transformations) — short-range sharing.
- The **atomic, molecular, nuclear bonds** — sharings at different scales.

**Mechanism B — Propagation of weaving within t=x.**
A weaving produces a signature that propagates at c within t=x, independently of proximity.
- **EM** (free, photon) — co-traveling weaving carrying energy and motif.
- **Gravity** (bottled) — propagated motif without energy (the energy stays at the address).

**Key structural distinction**:
- A requires durable proximity and at least one frozen carrier. Free is excluded from A by construction (its presence at a point is infinitesimal).
- B does not depend on proximity — it is a propagation, not a sharing.

**The "degree of impediment" of the initial version of Postulate IX** continues to play **inside B** (differentiates EM and gravity by the freedom of weaving to co-travel). But A is qualitatively something else, not a simple variation of impediment.

| Force | Mechanism | Regime | Range | Requirement |
|-------|-----------|--------|-------|-------------|
| **EM** | B | Free (co-traveling) | Infinite | — |
| **Gravity** | B | Bottled (motif) | Infinite (weak) | — |
| **Weak** | A | Sharing at t=0 | ~10⁻¹⁸ m | Proximity |
| **Strong** | A | Sharing at t=0 | ~10⁻¹⁵ m | Strong proximity |

**Immediate consequences**:
- The **graviton** as a distinct particle does not exist (consistent with initial version).
- The **gluon** is not a mediator particle — it is the **portion of back-and-forth shared** between neighboring quarks. Geometry, not object. Consistent with its non-particle behavior (self-interaction, never isolated).
- The **EM/gravity asymmetry** (EM cancels by neutrality, gravity does not) remains explained by the internal structure (charge in bottling, not in weaving).
- The **confinement** of quarks is structural: their self-coherent oscillation requires sharing at t=0 with a partner. Without partner, the form does not close — an isolated quark is not a physical particle.
